Welcome To The Heart Of Transylvania!

Welcome!

Welcome to Cluj-Napoca, the dynamic heart of Transylvania, Romania!

Immerse yourself in a city where medieval charm meets modern innovation. Explore historic landmarks like St. Michael's Church, wander through the lively Old Town, and discover a thriving cultural scene with festivals and events year-round.

As the "Silicon Valley of Romania", Cluj-Napoca embraces progress while maintaining its rich heritage. With a vibrant mix of history, technology, and youthful energy, this city invites you to experience a unique blend of tradition and innovation.

About Cluj-Napoca

Cluj-Napoca has a population of over 300,000 people - that makes it the second largest city in Romania by population - over an area of 179.5 squared meters, with an elevation of 360 meters. With hills and two forests, as well as squares, theaters and universities, it is a city of great diversity which could help very diverse people lead a fulfilling life.

Cluj-Napoca is located in the North-West of Romania, Europe.

Education and student life
A cool fact about the city is that it has the highest ratio of students to total number of residents in Romania, due to its seven state and three private universities. Find out more about education and student life in Cluj here.

Events and culture
Cluj-Napoca entered the network of UNESCO creative cities, with the title of City of Film, along with 20 other cities spread across the globe, thanks to the Transylvania International Film Festival taking place here, but not only. The city is known for its cultural diversity, with a mix of Romanian, Hungarian, and German influences. This is reflected in the city's architecture, cuisine, and traditions.
